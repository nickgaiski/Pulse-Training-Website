Before you book

Your questions, answered

I'm a total beginner — is Pulse really for me?

Absolutely. Most of our clients start exactly where you are. Pulse is known as a welcoming place for beginners — we meet you at your level, with zero experience required.

What actually happens on the free discovery call?

It's a relaxed, no-pressure conversation. We get to know you, listen to your goals, answer your questions, and figure out together whether Pulse is the right fit. No commitment and no hard sell — promise.

I'm nervous about walking into a gym.

You're in good company — "gym-xiety" is one of the most common things we hear. Pulse is a friendly, judgment-free space, and your coach is right beside you every step of the way.

Will I even have time for this as a busy mom?

We build your plan around your real life — not the other way around. Sustainable beats extreme every time, and your coach keeps it simple so it actually fits your week.
