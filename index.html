Why Pulse exists

It started with a mom who always put herself last.

In 2007, Jeff Weinacht started Pulse by training one person — his own mom. She was 55, a mother of three, and she'd spent her whole life taking care of everyone but herself. One year later, she was in better shape at 55 than she'd been at 40.

That's the whole idea behind Pulse. We're not a bro-gym. We're a personal-training studio in Highland, IL built for moms who've been putting themselves last — coached by people who've walked it themselves.

Nope. Most of our moms start exactly there. We meet you where you are and build up slowly — no judgment, ever.

No crash diets here. Just simple, steady changes and a coach who keeps you going — so it actually sticks this time.
